Korodhka waxaa kaxeeya magaaloobidda, hindisayaasha magaalooyinka caqliga leh, iyo kororka caalamiga ah ee dambiyada hantida, taasoo kordhisa baahida nidaamyada amniga caqliga leh ee firfircoon.

Falanqaynta AI ee horumarsan ayaa u oggolaanaysa dareemayaasha inay kala soocaan khataraha dhabta ah iyo kuwa been abuurka ah, iyadoo digniinaha aan loo baahnayn la dhimay ilaa 60%.

**Talo Media:** Ku dhex dar muuqaal kooban oo muujinaya nidaamyada alaarmiga smart IoT-Enabled ee ku shaqeynaya goobaha badan.

Iibsadayaasha tirada badan, tani waxay xaqiijineysaa rakibid la qabsan kara oo ka socda hawlgallada goobaha badan, sida bakhaaro ama silsiladaha hoteelada, iyadoo la adeegsanayo dashboards dhexe si loo ogaado waxyaabaha aan caadiga ahayn waqtiga dhabta ah.

### 2. Habayn iyo Dabacsanaanta OEM
Iibsadayaasha caalamiga ah waxay si kordhaya u dalbanayaan xalal OEM/ODM, iyagoo u habeynaya dareemayaasha, guddiyada xakamaynta, iyo module-yada isku xirka ee codsiyada gaarka ah.
