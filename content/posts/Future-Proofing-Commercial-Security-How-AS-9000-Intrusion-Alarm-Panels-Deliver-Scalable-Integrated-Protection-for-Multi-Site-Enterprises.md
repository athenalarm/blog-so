## Muuqaalka Amniga Ganacsiga ee Horumaraya

### Kakanaanta Khatarta ee Sii Kordhaysa ee Hawlaha Goobaha Badan Leh

Shirkadaha maanta waxay ka hawlgalaan magaalooyin, gobollo, iyo qaarado kala duwan — badanaana waxay maareeyaan tobanaan ama boqollaal goobood oo jidheed. Baaxaddan waxaa la socota khatar sare: gelitaanka aan la ogolayn, kharribaadda, xatooyada gudaha, iyo isku dayada soo gelitaanka ee casriga ah waxay ka dhici karaan isku mar goobo kala duwan. Nidaamyada digniinta ee dhaqameed, ee loogu talagalay meelaha yaryar ama dhismayaasha goonida ah, ma laha qaab-dhismeedka loo baahan yahay ee muuqaalka guud ee shirkadda iyo jawaab-celinta isku dubaridan, taas oo ka dhigaysa ururada kuwo u nugul carqaladaynta hawlgalka iyo khasaaraha maaliyadeed.

Amniga shirkadaha ee wax ku oolka ah wuxuu u baahan yahay nidaamyo ka gudba digniinaha goonida ah si ay u bixiyaan kormeer isku dhex jira, diiwaangelinta xogta, iyo [maamulka dhexe](https://athenalarm.com/network-alarm-system/network-alarm-monitoring-system-application/). Tani waa halka guddiyada soo gelitaanka ee casriga ahi ay u shaqeeyaan sidii noodhadh caqli-gal ah oo ku dhex jira nidaam amni oo ballaadhan.

Guddiga digniinta soo gelitaanka ma aha oo kaliya qalab kiciya seeriga. Deegaannada muhiimka ah — laga bilaabo xarumaha bangiyada ilaa xarumaha warshadaha — waxay u shaqeeyaan sidii xarunta taliska amniga: ururinta calaamadaha dareemayaasha, fulinta caqliga ansixinta digniinta, gudbinta dhacdooyinka guud ahaan shabakadaha, iyo isku xidhka goobaha kormeerka fog. Amniga shirkadaha, guddiyadu waa inay si fudud u fidi karaan, taageeraan kaabayaasha isku dhafan ee xadhigga leh/bilaa xadhigga ah, oo ay bixiyaan digniino wakhtiga dhabta ah iyada oo loo marayo kanaalada isgaadhsiinta ee kala duwan.

Qoyska AS-9000 waxay aqbalaan baahiyahan iyagoo bixinaya awood aag oo ballaadhan iyo isgaadhsiin dheeraad ah — sifooyinka qeexaya guddiyada digniinta soo gelitaanka ee heerka warshadaha ee casriga ah ee amniga shirkadaha.

Wadnaha nashqadda AS-9000 waa fidinteeda modular-ka ah. Guddi kasta oo saldhig ah wuxuu taageeraa 16 aag oo xadhig leh iyo 30 aag oo bilaa xadhig ah — taas oo ah baaxad aad uga weyn nidaamyada digniinta ganacsiyada yaryar ee caadiga ah. Fidintan waxay ka dhigan tahay in kooxaha iibka ay dejin karaan qaabayn bilow ah oo ku habboon baahiyaha degdegga ah ayna si dabiici ah u fidin karaan markii tas-hiilaadku koraan — iyada oo aan la beddelin qalabka asaasiga ah. Dabacsanaanta noocaas ah waxay dhigaysaa AS-9000 mid ka mid ah guddiyada digniinta soo gelitaanka ugu fiican ee ganacsiga ee diiradda saaraya u adkaysiga hawlgalka ee muddada dheer.

Guddiga AS-9000 wuxuu bixiyaa kiiboodh LCD ah oo horumarsan oo leh tilmaamo cod ah oo dareen leh iyo taageero luqado badan, isagoo abuuraya interface saaxiibtinimo u leh isticmaalaha labadaba habaynta iyo hawlgallada maalinlaha ah. AS-9000 micro-processor-ka 32-bit ARM wuxuu hubiyaa habaynta calaamadaha degdegga ah iyo ogaanshaha dhacdada ee la isku halayn karo iyada oo aan dib-u-dhac dhicin — arrin muhiim ah marka ilbiriqsi kasta uu xisaabsan yahay ogaanshaha soo gelitaanka. ### Dabacsanaanta Isgaadhsiinta ee PSTN, 4G, iyo TCP/IP

Kala duwanaanshaha weyn ee guddiyada heerka shirkadaha wuxuu ku jiraa dabacsanaanta isgaadhsiintooda. AS-9000 waxay taageertaa habab badan oo gudbin ah:

* PSTN (khadka telefoonka): Isgaadhsiin dhaqameed oo la isku halayn karo halka xidhiidhka TCP/IP laga yaabo in aan la helin.

* 4G (LTE): Isku xidhka gacanta wuxuu hubiyaa in dhacdooyinka digniinta la gudbiyo xitaa marka khadadka telefoonku xumaadaan — ku habboon goobaha fogfog ama tas-hiilaadka leh shabakadaha xadhigga leh ee aan deganayn.

* TCP/IP (Ethernet): Gudbinta ku salaysan shabakadda waxay awood u siinaysaa is-dhexgalka goobaha kormeerka dhexe iyo faafinta dhacdooyinka oo degdeg ah.

#### Doorka Guddiyada

Guddiyada sida AS-9000 waxay u shaqeeyaan sidii xarumaha taliska, iyagoo taageeraya kaabayaasha isku dhafan.

#### Diyaarinta Mustaqbalka

La qabsigu waa furaha ROI, iyada oo nashqadaha modular-ka ah ay ka hortagayaan dugowga.

### Astaamaha Muhiimka ah oo la Balaadhiyay

#### Qaab-dhismeedka Aagga

16 xadhig leh + 30 bilaa xadhig ah, oo loo fidin karo 1,656. Tani waxay u ogolaanaysaa koritaan dabiici ah iyada oo aan qalabka dib u habayn lagu samayn.

![Guddi digniineed oo heerka warshadaha ah oo faahfaahsan](https://athenalarm.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/Athenalarm-alarm-control-panel-1.jpg)

#### Fududaynta Xakamaynta

Kiiboodhka LCD, tilmaamaha codka, luqado badan. Ilaa 11 isticmaale oo loogu talagalay gelitaanka ku salaysan doorka.

#### Adkaysiga Habaynta

32-bit ARM, ilaalinta ka dhanka ah wareegyada gaaban, culayska xad-dhaafka ah, iyo faragelinta. Baaxadda hawlgalka: -10°C ilaa 55°C, 40-70% qoyaanka.

![Shahaadada guddiga digniinta ee heerka warshadaha](https://athenalarm.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Athenalarm-alarm-control-panel-IEC-62368-1.jpg)

Shahaadooyinka waxaa ka mid ah [Athenalarm CCC iyo IEC 62368-1](https://athenalarm.com/burglar-alarm/intrusion-alarm-panel/alarm-control-panel), iyadoo la hubinayo u-hoggaansanaanta.

### Faahfaahinta Isgaadhsiinta

#### Dabacsanaanta

PSTN, 4G (guutooyinka LTE ee la cayimay), TCP/IP. Moodooyinka: AS-9000FX (PSTN), AS-9000GPRS-4G (PSTN & 4G), AS-9000IP (PSTN & TCP/IP), AS-9000FF (PSTN & 4G & TCP/IP).

#### Geynta

Isku dar cutubyada si aad u hesho caymiska ugu wanaagsan.

#### Diiwaangelinta

1,500 oo dhacdo, taageerada daruuraha ee hantidhawrka.
