Haddii Wi-Fi-gu go'o—sababtoo ah koronto la'aan, carqaladaynta adeeg bixiyaha internet-ka (ISP), ama faragelinta calaamadaha—laf-dhabarta GSM/4G ayaa si aan kala go' lahayn ula wareegaysa, iyada oo hubinaysa in calaamadaha alaarmiga ay gaadhaan isticmaalaha ugu dambeeya iyada oo loo marayo kanaalo badan: ogeysiisyada app-ka, SMS, wicitaanada codka, ama xitaa soo sheegidda xarunta amniga ee intranet-ka.

Faa'iidada ganacsi waa mid degdeg ah: fariimaha digniinta ee aan bixin oo yaraada, dhibcaha qanacsanaanta isticmaalaha oo sareeya, iyo sumcad adag oo u soo hoyata qaybiyaha.
